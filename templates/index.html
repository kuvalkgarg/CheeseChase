CheeseChase | Automated Mouse Movement for Idle Users

A Tool to Overcome User Inactivity on Office Systems

When you click on the "Begin" button provided below, your mouse will begin to move around aimlessly in random directions, in order to simulate user activity, when you are away from your desk. To halt this simulation, hit the "X" key on your keyboard.

Safety Assurance:

  • To avoid closing any tabs, the mouse will remain well away from all corners of your screen.
  • The tool will not perform any operation other than moving the cursor around. Operations like clicking, dragging, etc. have not been included as a safeguard.
